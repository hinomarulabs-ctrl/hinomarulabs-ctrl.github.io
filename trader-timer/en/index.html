Guide

Why candle-close timing matters

A candle is not final until its interval closes. Before that moment, price can reverse, wicks can disappear, and a setup that looked clean can change completely. Waiting for the candle close helps many traders make decisions from confirmed data instead of reacting to noise.

Trader Timer is designed for that exact moment. It gives you audio timing cues for the intervals you care about, so you can stay focused on the chart, the plan, and the trade rather than manually checking the clock.

Use it beside TradingView or any charting platform. Whether you trade stocks, forex, CFDs, or crypto, the goal is simple: know when the next decision point arrives.

FAQ

What is Trader Timer?

Trader Timer is a trading timer app that helps you track candle closes and elapsed time with voice alerts and notifications.

Yes. You can create profiles and timers for different markets, chart intervals, and trading styles.

Does it support 1-minute, 5-minute, and 15-minute charts?

Yes. Trader Timer is built to complement charting tools, so your chart can stay front and center.

Does it support background notifications?

Yes, though delivery can depend on your device settings and operating-system notification rules.
