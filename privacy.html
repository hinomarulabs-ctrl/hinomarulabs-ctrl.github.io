Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

Hinomaru Labs (“we”, “our”, or “us”) develops and operates mobile applications, including but not limited to DayNav and Trader Timer.

This Privacy Policy explains how our applications handle your information.

1. Information Collection

Hinomaru Labs applications do not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information.

We do not require account registration.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or other personally identifiable information.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

Some application settings are stored locally on your device to provide app functionality.

Examples include:

  • schedules
  • timer settings
  • alert settings
  • voice settings
  • language settings
  • application preferences

These data remain on your device and are not transmitted to Hinomaru Labs.

3. Internet Communication

Our applications do not transmit your personal data to Hinomaru Labs.

Some operating system features (such as text-to-speech engines or system services) may communicate with third-party providers depending on your device configuration.

Such communications are governed by the privacy policies of the respective platform providers.

4. Third-Party Services

Hinomaru Labs applications do not use:

  • advertising networks
  • analytics services
  • user tracking technologies
  • behavioral profiling

If this changes in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Permissions

Depending on the application and operating system, permissions such as notifications may be requested solely to provide application functionality.

Permissions are not used to collect personal information.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our applications are not specifically directed toward children under the age required by applicable law.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any updates will be published on this page, and the “Last Updated” date will be revised accordingly.

8. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the official support page provided by Hinomaru Labs.
