RecallBloom Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

Hinomaru Labs (“we”, “our”, or “us”) develops and operates RecallBloom (“App”).

This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Information Collection

Hinomaru Labs does not directly collect, store, or share personal information through our own servers.

Some third-party services used in the App, such as Google AdMob, may process information as described in this Privacy Policy.

We do not require account registration.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or other personally identifiable information.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

Training history, Brain Scores, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device to provide App functionality.

Examples include:

  • training history;
  • Brain Score and statistics;
  • game settings;
  • sound settings;
  • language settings; and
  • application preferences.

These data remain on your device and are not transmitted to or stored on Hinomaru Labs servers.

Locally stored data is deleted from your device when you delete it within the App, reset the App data, or uninstall the App.

3. Internet Communication

The App does not transmit your personal data to Hinomaru Labs.

Some operating system features and third-party services may communicate with their providers depending on your device configuration.

Such communications are governed by the privacy policies of the respective providers.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use Google AdMob to display advertisements.

When advertisements are displayed, the App may use the Google Mobile Ads SDK. Google may collect and use information in accordance with its own policies.

This information may be used by Google for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention purposes.

For more information, please refer to the following Google pages:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use Google services

The App may offer in-app purchases through the Apple App Store or Google Play. Payment information such as credit card details is managed by Apple or Google. Hinomaru Labs does not obtain or store your payment information.

5. Permissions

Depending on the App and operating system, permissions such as notifications may be requested solely to provide App functionality.

Permissions are not used to collect personal information.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not specifically directed toward children under the age required by applicable law.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any updates will be published on this page, and the “Last Updated” date will be revised accordingly.

8. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the official contact form provided by Hinomaru Labs.
